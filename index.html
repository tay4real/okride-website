How It Works

Book a verified ride in three steps

OkRide makes getting a safe, verified motorcycle ride as easy as tapping a button.

1
📍
Enter your destination
Open OkRide, enter where you're going, and see all verified riders near you on the live map. Each rider shows their name, plate number, rating, and distance.
2
🏍️
Choose your rider
Select a rider and confirm the fare. Your rider gets an SMS notification and accepts the trip. You receive their plate number and ETA confirmation by SMS.
3
✅
Ride safely and pay
Track your trip live. Your family can watch too via a shared link. Pay automatically via Paystack when you arrive. Rate your rider after the trip.
Safety First

Built around passenger safety

OkRide was created specifically to address the security challenges facing Ondo State residents using motorcycle transport.

🆘
One-tap SOS emergency alert Instantly sends GPS coordinates to all emergency contacts and security agencies
🗺️
Route deviation detection Auto-alerts if the rider deviates from the agreed route by more than 200 metres
👁️
Family live tracking Share a real-time tracking link with family — no app needed on their end
⏱️
Trip timeout alerts If a trip takes 50% longer than expected, family contacts are notified automatically
🪪
NIN verification via NIMC Every rider's National Identification Number is verified directly with the National Identity Management Commission before activation.
🚔
Police background check All riders are cleared through Ondo State Police records. Any criminal history results in automatic rejection.
🦺
Physical ID vests Approved riders wear QR-coded OkRide vests. Passengers can scan to verify identity before boarding.
🔄
6-month re-verification Riders are re-screened every 6 months. Any new criminal flag results in immediate suspension.

Keeping you informed every step

OkRide uses SMS exclusively for transactional messages — security codes, trip updates, and safety alerts.

Booking Confirmation
"OkRide: Adewale Ojo is on the way. Plate: OND-442-KA. ETA: 5 mins. Rider is verified and background-checked."
Payment Receipt
"OkRide: Payment of NGN500 confirmed for your trip. Ref: TXN123456. Thank you for riding safely."
Emergency SOS Alert
"EMERGENCY: Amaka may be in danger. Location: maps.google.com/?q=7.25,5.20. Rider plate: OND-442-KA."

Strictly transactional SMS only

OkRide never sends promotional or marketing SMS. Every message is triggered by a real user action — logging in, booking a ride, completing a payment, or triggering an emergency alert.

All SMS is sent through Termii, a licensed Nigerian SMS provider, using the OkRide Sender ID.

  • OTP login verification codes
  • Trip booking confirmations
  • Rider arrival notifications
  • Payment receipts
  • Rider earnings notifications
  • Emergency SOS alerts to contacts
